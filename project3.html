Introduction

In the Summer of 2020, I received the opportunity to co-lead interaction design components of a research project between CSIRO's Data61 and UNSW's EPICentre, as Data61 Vacation Scholar.

Alongside a developer and HCI team, I co-lead a research project exploring Virtual Reality and its applications in Cybersecurity. My work at EPICentre has transformed the way I view design while I built on my technical skills as a developer and I am so grateful to all the people that guided me along the way.

Due to an NDA, I'm unable to disclose further information about the project. Feel free to connect with me to learn more about my experience!
